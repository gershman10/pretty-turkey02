MISSION

The purpose of this publication is the realization of human potential, today. It seeks not only to re- establish the values that are currently being degraded, but to push further and pick up where the previous pinnacles of human achievement have left off. The approach will be multi-faceted: a combination of fact-based, empirically-tested solutions that can be executed the moment you put down an article; longer-term conversations and understandings to found this realization; and examples of this realized potential in arts and sciences already upon us today. For decades, the time has been ripe for us to enter a true, non-gilded tomorrow. Yet, we somehow find ourselves scrambling for pieces of what we had yesterday. This publication seeks to re-align our energies away from that which only allows us survival, and towards that which bursts us forth into the new era.
